Refund Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

📦 Quick Summary: Digital products are non-refundable once accessed, but we offer refunds for technical issues, duplicates, or if you're not satisfied within 14 days. We're reasonable — just reach out.

1. Digital Products Policy

Due to the nature of digital products (instant download, non-returnable):

  • Downloaded products: Generally non-refundable once accessed
  • Unaccessed products: Refundable within 14 days of purchase
  • Technical issues: Full refund if we cannot resolve the problem
  • Duplicate purchases: Full refund for accidental duplicate charges

2. Eligible Refund Scenarios

2.1 Technical Issues

  • Download links not working
  • Files corrupted or inaccessible
  • Product doesn't work as described
  • Missing components or pages

Process: Contact us first — we'll try to fix it. If we can't, full refund.

2.2 Duplicate Charges

  • Accidental double purchase
  • Payment processing errors

Process: Email us with order details. We'll refund the duplicate within 24 hours.

2.3 Not As Described

  • Product significantly different from description
  • Missing features advertised

Process: Contact us within 14 days. We'll either fix it or refund.

2.4 Subscription Cancellations

  • Cancel anytime before next billing cycle
  • No partial refunds for mid-cycle cancellations
  • Access continues until end of paid period

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds are typically NOT provided for:
  • Change of mind after accessing the product
  • Not using the product after purchase
  • Already downloaded/accessed files (unless defective)
  • Purchases made more than 14 days ago
  • Free products or pay-what-you-want items

4. How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Email us at harshaldevloper@gmail.com

Step 2: Include the following information:

  • Order number (from Paddle confirmation email)
  • Product name
  • Reason for refund request
  • Any relevant screenshots or details

Step 3: We'll respond within 2-3 business days

Step 4: If approved, refund processed within 5-10 business days

5. Refund Timeline

  • Response time: 2-3 business days
  • Approval decision: Within 5 business days
  • Refund processing: 5-10 business days (via Paddle)
  • Bank processing: Additional 3-5 business days (varies by bank)

Total time: Typically 10-20 business days from request to funds in your account

6. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Bundle purchases (refund for specific items)
  • Subscription cancellations (pro-rated, at our discretion)
  • Minor issues that don't warrant full refund

7. Chargebacks

Important: Please contact us before filing a chargeback with your bank or Paddle.

Chargebacks without prior contact may result in:

  • Permanent ban from future purchases
  • Revocation of product access
  • Additional fees charged to you

We're reasonable and want to help — just reach out first.

8. Promotional or Free Products

  • Free products: No refunds (no payment made)
  • Discounted products: Refund limited to amount paid
  • Bonus items: Must be returned if main product refunded

9. Currency & Exchange Rates

Refunds are processed in the original payment currency:

  • Exchange rate fluctuations are not covered
  • You receive the exact amount paid (in original currency)
  • Bank conversion fees are your responsibility

10. Exceptions

We reserve the right to make exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis. If you have extenuating circumstances, please contact us and we'll review your situation fairly.

11. Contact

For refund requests or questions:

  • Email: harshaldevloper@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Order Number]"
  • Response Time: 2-3 business days

12. Policy Changes

We may update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. The policy in effect at the time of purchase applies to your order.

💬 We're Human, Not Robots:
This policy is a guideline, not a rigid rule. If you have a legitimate issue, we'll work with you. Just be honest and reach out — we want you to be happy with your purchase.
